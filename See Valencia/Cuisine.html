Restaurant Navarro

This dish is a must try in Valencia –
It is called Paella, it is a worldwide famous rice dish that
is served in a shallow pan and packed with flavors of saffron and spices.
Our favorite place to go is Restaurante Navarro. Add Tapas to add variety to your meal.

Location: Restaurante Navarro - Calle Arzobispo Mayoral, 5, 46002 Valencia, Spain +34 963 52 96 23
