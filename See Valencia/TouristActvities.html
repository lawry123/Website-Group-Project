Valencia has plenty of beautiful traditional sand beaches,
lodging, and transportation!

Follow our site for recommendations and tips on the tourist activities in Valencia.

La Malvarrosa Beach

Located just North of the municipal district, this fine
sand beach is wide and ready for relaxation.

Location: Passeig de Neptu, 30456 Valencia, Spain
Photo By VisitValencia / CC BY 2.0

El Saler Beach

Chill in the sand at El Saler! Enjoy
the clear water and long walks on this uncommercialised beach area.

Location: Calle Eugenia Vines 40, 46011 Valencia, Spain
El Saler Playa by Christopher Økæ / CC BY 2.0

Port Saplaya Beach

Walk along the boardwalk on this populated beach!

Location: Port Saplaya Beach, Valencia, Spain
North Beach by PlayaValencia / CC BY 2.0

Lodging

Hotel San Lorenzo Boutique

Wake up in authentic Valencian style when
you rent a hotel room for as low as £24 a person at Hotel San Lorenzo Boutique.

Location: San Lorenzo, 2, 46001 Valencia, Spain
Hotel San Lorenzo Boutique by Destinia / CC BY 2.0

Vincci Lys

In the heart of Valencia is the Vincci Lys Hotel,
a convienient distance from the train station and Town Hall Square.

Location: Carrer de Martínez Cubells, 5, 46002 València, Spain
